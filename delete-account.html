Account Deletion - HUGE%

This page provides instructions for requesting deletion of your account and associated data in the HUGE% application, published by HUGE% on Google Play.

Steps to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to our support team at huge@huge-support-app.com from the same email address you used to register your account.
  2. Include the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. In the email body, please confirm your username (if applicable) and request deletion of your account and associated data.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account information (username, email address).
  • User-generated content (e.g. uploaded media, profile details).
  • Any other personal data associated with your account.

Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period of time if required by law, for legitimate business purposes (such as security, fraud prevention, or financial reporting), or to comply with regulatory requirements. Such data will be permanently deleted once the retention period expires.

Contact

If you have questions regarding account deletion, please contact us at huge@huge-support-app.com.
