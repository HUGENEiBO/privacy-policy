Privacy Policy

This application requires access to certain device features in order to provide its core functionality. Below is a detailed explanation of why each permission is requested:

  • Location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION, ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION): Used to enable location-based features. Location data is processed locally and not shared with third parties.
  • Internet (INTERNET): Required to connect the app with online services and display content.
  • Storage & Media (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO): Used to read and display images and videos stored on the device. No files are uploaded without explicit user consent.
  • Camera (CAMERA): Used strictly for capturing photos or videos within the app.
  • NFC (NFC): Used for reading NFC tags when available.
  • Vibration (VIBRATE): Used to provide device feedback on certain actions.

Data Usage

The app does not sell, share, or misuse personal information. All data collected through the listed permissions is used solely to enable the intended features of the application.

Third Parties

No user data is transferred to third parties. Any information processed by the app remains on the user’s device unless explicitly required for the app’s functionality and with user consent.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data accessed by the app. However, please note that no system can be guaranteed as completely secure.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Users will be informed of significant changes through updates to this document.
